Educational Explanation

What is Credential Stuffing? It is the reuse of leaked usernames and passwords to try many accounts quickly. Attackers rely on humans reusing passwords across services.

Why MFA protects accounts? Even if a password is guessed or reused, MFA adds another verification factor and reduces the chance of compromise.

This is a controlled educational demo only. It does not target any real service or account.
